about

what I actually do

Minecraft server engineering is the center of my work: plugin systems, backend logic, network tooling, deployment, and the internal interfaces needed to keep everything usable.

identity

I’m a Minecraft server engineer who cares about the internals and the surface. I like building systems that survive real use, but I also care about staff tools, dashboards, and public-facing interfaces feeling sharp.

That mix is what I want the site to communicate. Not “generic full-stack,” but someone who can ship gameplay systems, operating tools, and the web layer around them.

operating principles

reliability first

cool features are worthless if the server stack is brittle under load or hard to debug.

staff experience matters

admin tools, moderation flows, and deployment surfaces should be fast and obvious.

make the system legible
